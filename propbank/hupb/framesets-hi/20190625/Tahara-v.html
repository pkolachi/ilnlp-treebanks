Roleset id: Tahar.03 , to make to stay, lodge/ to make a ruling or decision

Tahar.03: Transitive- sense1/ sense2

Roles:

        Arg0: entity arranging the stay/ the one who makes the decision or ruling
        Arg1: entity staying/ the decision or the ruling that is made
        Arg2-loc: location of stay/ where the ruling is made

Example: to make to stay

        taharA
        बाबुजी ने मेहमानों को घर पर ठहराया

        Arg0: बाबुजी ने (drel: k1)
        Arg1: मेहमानों को (drel: k2)
        Arg2-loc: घर पर (drel: k7p)
        Rel: ठहराया
