Predicate: nahA

nahA: created by Archna Bhatia

Roleset id: nahA.01 , to bathe (clean body with water usually)/ to be drenched (in sweat)

nahA.01: Intransitive- sense1_HindiWN/ sense2_HindiWN

Roles:

        Sometimes an ARG3 (k3) is also used, e.g. "xUXa se" in "xUXa se nahAnA". For the sense "to be drenched in sweat", "sweat" gets ARG3 (k3) label.
        Arg0: the bather (the one who bathes)/ the one who is/gets drenched (in sweat)
        Arg3: the material used for taking bath, such as water/ the sweat

Example: intransitive unergative verb

        आतिफ बाद में नहायेगा
        AwiPZa bAxa meM nahAegA

        Arg0: आतिफ (drel: k1)
        Argm-tmp: बाद में (drel: k7t)
        Rel: नहायेगा

Example: obligation construction- dative subject infinitive V

        आतिफ को नहाना है
        AwiPZa ko nahAnA hE

        Arg0: आतिफ को (drel: k1)
        Rel: नहाना है

Example: intransitive- sense2_HindiWN (to be drenched in sweat)

        http://deepakraj.wordpress.com/category/web-duniya/ sentence modified a bit
        चार मजदूर गरमी की दोपहर तेज धूप में पसीने से नहा रहे थे

        Arg0: चार मजदूर (drel: k1)
        Argm-loc: गरमी की दोपहर तेज धूप में (drel: k7)
        Arg3: पसीने से (drel: k3)
        Rel: नहा रहे थे
