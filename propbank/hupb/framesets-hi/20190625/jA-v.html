Roleset id: jA.01 , self directed motion

jA.01: The verb tests as unaccusative but it can take animate subjects. However, it doesn't pass an unergative test.*Raam ne gaya is not possible. Another motion verb like aana 'come' has the same problem. Here we will split self-directed motion and motion into two different rolesets like English, where self directed takes Arg0, and motion takes Arg1

Roles:

        Arg0: goer, entity in motion
        Arg2-gol: location of motion

Example:

        राम घर गया

        Arg0: राम (drel: k1)
        Arg2-gol: घर (drel: k2p)
        Rel: गया

Roleset id: jA.02 , motion or path taken by the entity in motion

jA.02: See jA.01

Roles:

        Arg1: entity in motion, goer
